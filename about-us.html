About Striders


Striders revolutionizes shoe customization by introducing versatile charms that can attach to any type of shoe, breaking away from the limitations set by traditional options like Crocs' Jibbitz. While Jibbitz allowed for personalization, their application was restricted to just the Croc and the front toe box area only. Striders expands on this concept by offering the freedom to attach charms anywhere on your footwear, allowing for a personal touch. With Striders, every pair of shoes becomes a canvas for self-expression, allowing individuals to transform their footwear into something extremely unique.
