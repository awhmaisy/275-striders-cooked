How it Works

    • Pin Back: This foundational element is inserted from the inside of the shoe, piercing through the fabric so only its shaft protrudes outward. The base remains inside, securing the pin in place.

      Pin Shield: Accompanying the Pin Back, the Pin Shield is a protective cover that facilitates the piercing process. It guides the Pin Back through the fabric safely and efficiently, and is removed once the pin is in position.

      Pin Body: The Pin Body is the backing of the decorative element that displays the design. It attaches from the outer side of the shoe, connecting to the Pin Back's shaft that extends through the fabric. Designed for a snug fit, it ensures the decoration stays secure and is easy to attach, allowing for quick customization.
